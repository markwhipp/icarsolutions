ECU Re-mapping

Here at Ingenious Car Solutions, we are specialists in ECU remapping for the Wickford area. If you feel that your vehicle needs that added boost of power or perhaps you’re having code-related problems such as DPF warnings, speed limiters and EGR issues, we can assist. We have a specialist technician based at our workshop, who can modify your cars software to unlock hidden power and fix costly issues.

The Process of ECU Re-mapping

  • Vehicle Diagnostics and Testing – before undertaking any work, we will need to ensure that the vehicle is ready and safe to be re-mapped. Therefore, we will run diagnostics, a vehicle health check and also test drive the vehicle to ensure safety.
  • Software Download – once we are happy that the car is ready to be re-mapped, we will download the original software on your vehicles ECU.
  • Software Modification – once the file is downloaded, we will amend the software and coding (to amend the way the ECU transmits to various components).
  • Software Upload – we will then take our modified file and upload it onto the vehicles ECU.
  • Final Tests – after the modified ECU file has been uploaded, we will test drive the vehicle to ensure we are happy with the modifications to the software and lastly run another diagnostics test.

We will only enhance your engine to what it’s components are capable of handling. We follow strict guidelines and proven methods of tuning to ensure that once your vehicle is remapped, it’ll be safe and worry-free. We perform both a before and after road test and diagnostics to ensure safe tuning.

Remember: We have a specialist technician based at our workshop and have the latest vehicle equipment and software for ECU re-mapping!
